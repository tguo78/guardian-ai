Invisible protection against cyberbullying — ethical, local and real-time.

The Problem

Cyberbullying has evolved with our digital lives. It's not always public or visible — often it hides in private messages, subtle exclusions, or repeated microaggressions that slowly isolate and damage. While early internet days saw around 19% of teens affected, today, over half experience cyberbullying, with some studies showing over 60% depending on gender.

The hardest part? Perception. What one person sees as a joke, another experiences as trauma. Exclusion, impersonation, hateful messaging — the forms vary, but the psychological toll is real. And traditional solutions like education, moderation, or reporting are often too slow, too late, or too easy to bypass.

Our Solution

GuardianAI is a new kind of safety net: a silent, respectful layer of protection directly on the device. Built with on-device AI, GuardianAI analyzes messages in real time — without sending any data to the cloud, ensuring complete privacy.

It doesn’t watch, it doesn’t judge — it listens. When it detects a message with clear signs of potential harm, it can suggest reflection, block the message, or gently notify a trusted adult if pre-approved. All decisions stay on the device, invisible to others.

GuardianAI aims to act at the critical moment — before harm occurs. It’s not punishment. It’s digital empathy, embedded at the right place, at the right time.
